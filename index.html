Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly are dermal fillers made of? ▾
Dermal fillers typically contain hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance in the skin that hydrates and plumps it.
How long do dermal fillers last?▾
The duration of dermal fillers varies but generally lasts between 6 months to 2 years, depending on the type of filler and area treated.
Is the treatment painful? ▾
Most dermal filler treatments involve minimal discomfort, as numbing creams or local anesthesia are often used.
Are there any side effects? ▾
Some common side effects include redness, swelling, and mild bruising, which typically subside within a few days.
How soon will I see results? ▾
Results are usually visible immediately after the treatment, with full effects noticeable once swelling subsides.
